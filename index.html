Recovery Coaching Survey Memo Pages

Updated: April 4, 2024

The purpose of this is memo is to provide the findings to AmeriCorps on the surveys distributed to recovery coaches, program directors, and program participants (i.e. the beneficiaries of recovery coaching programming) during the course of the Recovery Coaching Bundled Evaluation project.
The surveys sought to assess the programmatic activities, and the potential changes in outcomes such as behaviors, attitudes, skills, and recovery capital for program participants and recovery coaches. Survey data were collected between November 2021 - January 2024 for 11 AmeriCorps-supported organizations. These 11 organizations and their key geographic area of programmatic activity are:
  1. Above and Beyond Family Recovery Center - Chicago area
  2. Foundation for Recovery - Nevada
  3. Healing Action Network - St. Louis, MO area
  4. Recovery Corps - Minnesota and Illinois
  5. Align9 - Tennessee
  6. County of Washington - West Virginia and Ohio
  7. Covenant Community - Atlanta, GA
  8. Footprints - Kansas City, MO
  9. Maggie's Place - Phoenix, AZ
  10. NYC Peer Corps - New York City, NY
  11. RHOPE - Richmond, VA

The final sample size for reach respondent type included:
  • Program Participants (N = 83)
  • Recovery Coaches (N = 85)
  • Program Directors (N = 26)
