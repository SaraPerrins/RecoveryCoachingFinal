Lived Experience for Coaches

How important is it for a potential Peer Recovery Supporter to possess lived experience? The response choices were on a Likert Scale from 1- Very Important to 5- Very Unimportant. We had 5 missing responses, and N = 20 reported “Very Important” and only N = 1 reported “Very Unimportant”. No program directors reported any other response option, so the program directors that provided a response felt strongly one way or another about lived experience for coaches.
